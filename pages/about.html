About Our Department

The Computer Science Department at the University for Development Studies is committed to excellence in education, research, and innovation. As part of the Faculty of Physical Sciences, we provide a comprehensive education that prepares students for successful careers in the rapidly evolving field of technology.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education in computer science and related fields, fostering innovation and preparing students to become leaders in technology and research.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in computer science education and research, contributing to technological advancement and societal development.

Department History

The Computer Science Department was established in the year 2023 in the University for Development Studies following the separation of the Navrongo campus of UDS. The Department became famous following the high number of students it always admit making it one of the largest department at the UDS NYC

The department was established with the goal of providing quality education in computer science and has grown to become one of the leading departments in the Faculty of Physical Sciences.
